Task 3: Car Price Prediction using Machine Learning

Key Insights

1.  Present Price is one of the strongest factors affecting Selling
    Price.
2.  Car Age negatively impacts resale value.
3.  Fuel Type and Transmission influence market value.
4.  Feature engineering improved model performance.
5.  Random Forest generally delivered stronger prediction accuracy.
6.  Data cleaning improved reliability.

Summary

Machine Learning models were used to predict car prices and identify the
most important pricing factors.
